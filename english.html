About NTTU

NTTU is a public university with more than 4,000 students, 40,000 alumni, and 728 faculty and staff.

NTTU is the premier center of higher education in the Taitung area.

We bring groundbreaking ideas to industry and assist in the innovation and “greening” of local businesses.

Our award-winning faculty members are renowned for working closely with students to solve major scientiﬁc,

Otechnological and societal challenges.

We put a strong emphasis on creating things—from art to bio-energy systems.
